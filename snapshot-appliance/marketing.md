# One-Page Product Overview

## Product Name
GCP Auto Snapshot Appliance

## Summary
A lightweight VM-based automation engine that manages persistent disk snapshots across your GCP environment. Designed for simplicity, reliability, and minimal overhead.

## Key Benefits
- Set-and-forget snapshots  
- Zero maintenance  
- Fully contained in your GCP project  
- Does not require external services  
- Tag-based filtering  
- Automatic cleanup  

## Ideal For
- Small and medium businesses  
- Developers or teams without dedicated SRE staff  
- Environments that need automated backups without the complexity of enterprise tools
